ML Engineering Pipeline

How We Build AI Systems

01

Data Collection & Labelling

Audit existing data sources, define labelling schema, use semi-supervised labelling tools (Label Studio, Scale AI) to create ground-truth datasets.

02

Feature Engineering

Data cleaning, normalisation, embeddings, feature selection (SHAP, permutation importance) and dimensionality reduction (PCA, UMAP).

03

Model Training & Selection

Experiment tracking in MLflow, hyperparameter tuning (Optuna), cross-validation, and model selection across tree-based, deep learning, and transformer architectures.

04

Evaluation & Explainability

Precision/recall/F1 analysis, confusion matrices, SHAP explanations for regulatory compliance, and bias auditing on protected attributes.

05

Deployment & Serving

FastAPI inference servers, Docker containers, auto-scaling on AWS SageMaker or GCP Vertex AI, with A/B shadow deployment for zero-risk rollout.

06

Monitoring & Retraining

Data drift detection (Evidently AI), model performance alerting, automated retraining triggers, and model versioning with DVC and MLflow registry.
